Projects

Below you’ll find four of my projects. Some of them are school assignments, and in a few cases the styling is minimal since CSS was not always a requirement. The "Todo List TypeScript" project reflects my personal style and approach more, while the others can be seen more as experiments where I explored different types of designs and techniques.

I’ve also worked on several other projects outside of these examples. For instance, I participated in a school competition called "Chas Challenge", where we worked in groups of 9–11 people with a mix of full-stack developers, DevOps engineers, and UX designers. My team chose to create an application entirely from scratch rather than taking on a predefined company assignment. We developed a consultant bank, where the features and interface varied depending on whether the user logged in as an administrator, sales representative, or consultant. Unfortunately, I no longer have access to this project, so I’m unable to showcase it here.
